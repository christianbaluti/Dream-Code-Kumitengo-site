About Us

What's behind the Brand?

Kumitengo Farm is a family-owned and operated agricultural enterprise dedicated to sustainable farming practices and the production of high-quality tree seedlings and organic produce. Located in the heart of Malawi, our farm spans over a collective 20 acres of fertile land, where we cultivate a diverse range of fruits, vegetables, herbs while encompassing a beautiful natural forest. In addition, we support a variety of rabbits and birds providing us with the much-needed organic manure and organic pesticide.

Our Team

The main reason why we are special

Our team consists of experienced farmers, agronomists, and agricultural technicians who are passionate about sustainable agriculture and dedicated to ensuring the success of Kumitengo Farms and contribute positively to the health of our environment.

Our Mission

Our mission at Kumitengo Farm is to demonstrate development and management of food forests while providing our community with fresh, nutritious, and sustainably grown produce. .

Our Vision

Our Vision is to create a sustainable agricultural ecosystem that enriches our community and environment, fostering a healthier, greener, and more prosperous future for all.

Our Motto

Our motto is Cultivating Sustainability, Nurturing Community.
